Frequently Asked Questions

The following are some of the commonly asked questions regarding our system.

Q1.

What is BITBo-iHealth System?

BITBo-iHealth System is a platform designed to assist in the management of health services in the Philippines at the Barangay level, which is the smallest administrative division in the country. BITBo-iHealth is a web-based system that helps in organizing health-related data and in monitoring health programs and services.

Q2.

Who is eligible to register for this system?

The BITBo-iHealth system is exclusively designed to cater the health management needs of residents within the area of Commonwealth, Quezon City area. This is tailor-made to assist Barangay officials and healthcare professionals in delivering top-notch healthcare services to the community. So, if you reside within this vibrant community and require access to modern health management tools, the BITBo-iHealth system is the perfect solution for you.

Q3.

What are the services offered in BITBo-iHealth System?

As of now, the available services on the BITBo-iHealth system are clinic services and Vaccination Services.

Q4.

How to Register?

In order to register, simply click on the "Register Here" button. You will then be redirected to a registration link where you can proceed to sign up.

Q5.

Is there a verification before successfuly creating an account?

Yes, to verify that you are a resident of Commonwealth, Quezon City, the system will ask you to go through a verification process. This process involves providing a valid Identification Card (ID), which will be utilized by the Barangay officials to confirm your residency. Additionally, you will need to take a selfie holding your ID card. The type of ID required may vary depending on the specifications of the Barangay.

Q6.

How can I schedule an appointment?

Before you can proceed with selecting a service, ensure that you have an account. Once you have an account, you can proceed to choose the desired service by providing the required information.

Q7.

Is there a fee to book a vaccination appointment?

Certainly! Our system provides a free of charge appointment scheduling service for all users who wish to avail of various services offered by the barangay.
